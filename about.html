Who We Are

The Ministry of Information, Culture and Ethical Orientation is responsible for communicating government activities to the public, preserving cultural heritage, and promoting ethical values across Gombe State.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To ensure effective dissemination of government information and promote cultural values.

Our Vision

To build an informed, united and ethically responsible society.
