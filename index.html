About the Ministry

What the Ministry Does

The Ministry is the government’s main channel for public communication, cultural development, information management, and ethical orientation. It helps citizens stay informed, values stay strong, and government actions stay visible.

Information Dissemination

News gathering, reporting, public enlightenment, and documentation of government activities.

Public Relations

Media relations, press briefings, reputation management, and crisis communication.

Culture

Cultural preservation, festivals, heritage protection, and creative industry development.

Ethical Orientation

Civic education, patriotism campaigns, value reorientation, and social responsibility advocacy.

Why It Matters

The Ministry’s Strategic Importance

Bridges Government and Citizens

It helps government communicate clearly and helps citizens understand policies and activities.

Builds Public Trust

Regular, accurate communication improves transparency and confidence in government.

Preserves Identity

It protects culture, heritage, and values that define the people of Gombe State.

Supports Democracy

It encourages citizen participation, awareness, and democratic engagement.

Promotes Accountability

It publicizes projects and activities that strengthen openness in government.

Drives Value Reorientation

It promotes ethical responsibility, patriotism, and responsible citizenship.

Development Support

Supporting the State’s Growth Agenda

The ministry helps publicize development projects, promote investment opportunities, encourage citizen participation, and strengthen accountability across sectors.

  • Publicizes government programmes and achievements
  • Encourages citizen engagement and public ownership
  • Supports communication on health, education, agriculture, and infrastructure
  • Promotes social trust and sustainability
